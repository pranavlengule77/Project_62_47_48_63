About Us

Pold Hospitals is the largest chain of hospitals in Maharashtra. It is the brainchild of Dr. Abc Xyz, one of India’s most renowned Neurosurgeons and more importantly an ardent practitioner of ethical medical practices. It was his philosophy to make quality healthcare available, accessible, & accountable and not restrict as a privilege to the few, which still stands as the group’s pivotal touchstone.

The Pold group has 8 Hospitals with more than 900 beds and 200 ICU beds. Currently we have more than 2000 clinicians along with 2600 supporting staff. Pold Hospitals has touched the lives of more than 50 lakh people by providing quality care.

Our Pold hospitals are easy to reach due to their strategic locations in the cities of Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur and Nashik.
